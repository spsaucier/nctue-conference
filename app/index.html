Better Together
2015 NCTUE Conference

With an extensive database of shared insights, our NCTUE members are better together.

Some things are just better together. Wine complements cheese, movies are more fun with popcorn and even bees and honey belong together. At NCTUE, we believe we’re better together too.

Come and learn how you can improve your organization with Q&As, breakout sessions, networking events and more at our annual NCTUE conference. Hosted at The Ritz Carlton Lodge on Lake Oconee, this conference will give you a chance to network with other industry professionals, learn more about mitigating risk and, of course, kick back and have fun.

At this conference, you’ll take advantage of informative speeches by experts including the President of Equifax’s U.S. Consumer Information Solutions as well as specialized sessions focusing on telecommunications and utilities. In addition, the NCTUE Board of Directors will offer tips to help you make the most of your NCTUE membership, maximize ROI and more.

You’ll also have the opportunity to play golf, relax at the spa, share meals with NCTUE members and even celebrate your newfound knowledge at a speakeasy-themed casino.



If you haven’t already joined NCTUE, this is a great opportunity for you to meet with members and get first-hand knowledge about the benefits of membership.

Remember to keep your calendar open, and register before the deadline on October 2nd! We can’t wait to show you why we’re better together with NCTUE.
