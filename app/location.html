Better Together
2015 NCTUE Conference

With an extensive database of shared insights, our NCTUE members are better together.

When you’re not attending Q&A sessions or networking breakfasts at the NCTUE conference, you’ll have time to relax and refresh at the luxurious Ritz Carlton hotel right on Lake Oconee. With a stunning lake view and convenient amenities, there’s plenty to enjoy both at and away from the conference.

The Ritz offers:
- Full-service spa and fitness center
- Golf courses
- Indoor and outdoor pool
- Scheduled transportation
- 24-hour concierge services
- Flat-panel television and wireless access

Booking your hotel room

As an NCTUE conference attendee, you are responsible for the payment of your room’s nightly rate, which is which is $219 plus 12 percent state taxes and a $40 activity fee. Equifax will be reserving each registered attendee’s room for the duration of the conference, however if you plan on extending your stay please contact the hotel directly.

For your convenience, a shuttle is scheduled on Tuesday to drive attendees from the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport to the Ritz Carlton hotel departing at 10 a.m., 12:30 p.m. and 3:00 p.m. If you plan on taking the shuttle, be sure to indicate which time on our registration page.
